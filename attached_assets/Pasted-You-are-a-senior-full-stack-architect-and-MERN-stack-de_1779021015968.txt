CORE GOAL
Build an EDI process flow manager for SERMACROPS Manufacturing that supports outbound and inbound EDI transactions, partner endpoint management, a document builder for X12 files, delivery validation, and centralized inbound receipt handling.

BUSINESS FLOW
The system follows an order-to-cash process starting from the Coffee Shop sending EDI 850 Purchase Order and ending with EDI 810 Invoice.

- Logistics

Inbound documents supported:
- EDI 850 – Purchase Order
